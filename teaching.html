Teaching Philosophy

I believe in fostering curiosity and critical thinking in students by connecting theoretical concepts with real-world applications. My approach emphasizes hands-on learning, particularly in experimental physics, where students can observe quantum phenomena and understand the underlying principles through direct experience.
