About

I am a PhD student at IISER Bhopal working on two-dimensional coherent spectroscopy and exciton dynamics under the supervision of Dr. Rohan Singh. My research focuses on developing advanced spectroscopic techniques for understanding light-matter interactions at the quantum level.

Research Focus: Frequency comb spectroscopy, ultrafast pulse shaping, and machine learning applications in spectroscopy.

Research Interests

  • Frequency comb spectroscopy for high-resolution metrology and rapid spectral mapping
  • Probing material properties using temporally and spatially structured ultrafast pulses
  • Multidimensional coherent spectroscopy for investigating excitonic dynamics
  • Neural networks for lineshape analysis in non-exponential decay dynamics
  • Integration of orbital angular momentum (OAM) modes in spectroscopy
